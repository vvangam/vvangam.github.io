Efficient Diagnosis of AMD Using AI

Technologies: ResNet-18, DenseNet-121, Harvard FairVision Dataset

Developed a deep learning pipeline achieving 89% accuracy and an AUC-ROC of 0.88.

Read More

  • Experimented with DenseNet-121, ResNet-50, and MobileNetV2; applied dropout (p=0.5) for regularization.
